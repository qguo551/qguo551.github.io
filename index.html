Research direction

What I am building toward

Multimodal MRI

I am interested in how different MRI-derived measurements can be combined to describe brain structure, function, and individual variability.

MRI physiology

A central theme is the physiological interpretation of MRI signals and the care needed when moving from image-derived features to biological claims.

Brain-computer interface

I follow computational and neurotechnology approaches that connect brain measurements with adaptive systems and interpretable analysis pipelines.

Working principles

Careful methods before impressive-looking outputs.

  • Keep research claims tied to verified data, metadata, and assumptions.
  • Separate exploratory ideas from confirmatory analysis and final reporting.
  • Make code, parameters, and interpretation readable enough to be checked.
