Research positioning

Starting this research blog

May 10, 2026 · Qiwei Guo

This site is the public entry point for my research notes as a PhD candidate at the Centre for Cognitive and Brain Sciences, University of Macau. My current work is organized around multimodal MRI, MRI physiology, and brain-computer interface.

I want this blog to do a modest but useful job: make my research thinking easier to inspect. Some posts will be short notes on methods, data interpretation, or software practice. Others will summarize project decisions, reading notes, and reusable computational workflows.

Research line

The main question behind the site is how quantitative and computational methods can help us understand brain structure, brain function, and their interactions without losing sight of physiology, measurement assumptions, and reproducibility.

Writing policy

I will keep speculative ideas separate from established results. Publication claims, quantitative conclusions, and method recommendations should be tied to verifiable sources or clearly labeled as working notes.

中文说明

这个博客会作为我的研究者入口，用来整理多模态磁共振、磁共振生理、脑机接口以及可复现计算分析相关的笔记。早期内容会先强调研究定位和方法思考，后续再逐步补充项目、论文和更完整的研究材料。
